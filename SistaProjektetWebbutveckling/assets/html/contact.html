One downstairs houses, among other things, a bunch of game developers, four friends with background on the producer education Culture, Society and Media Design, KSM, at Campus Norrköping. In June, they launch their computer game "Champions of Aerial". When Folkbladet meets two of the guys, Martin Torstensson, 28, and Johannes Öhlin, 22, they tell that they want back to the feeling of video games, where you sit next to each other, but here with a game for PC. They describe the game as the master of the plot, with four players on a playing field shrinking, where it is necessary to put out the others. You sit in front of the same screen, with each control. A company is underway, named Quick Save Studios. The gaming initiative has been initiated by Martin Torstensson. - I want a profession where I can wake up in the morning and feel that it is fun to go to work, he says.
